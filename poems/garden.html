will You forgive me
for trying to plant my heart
in another garden?

i tried growing some roses
but no veins wanted to root,
considered other flowers and fruits
but they were all doomed

for with the emission
of Your earth and nutrition
there can't be transmission
of seeds, from heart to heart
nothing can exist, from end to start

now that i am wiser
i'm begging You to reconsider
showing me Your garden, opening Your gates
allow me to sow my heart to grow
a bit bigger, a bit thicker
a lot closer to my fate
